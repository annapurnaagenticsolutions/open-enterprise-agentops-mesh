# v1.6 Forward Roadmap — Connector Contract SDK and Dry-Run Adapters

After v1.5, the platform has identity simulation, secret references, approvals, audit, and sandbox execution.

The next safe step is not live connectors. The next safe step is a connector contract SDK.

## v1.6 proposed scope

- connector adapter interface,
- dry-run connector adapter examples,
- connector capability manifest,
- rollback/compensation contract template,
- rate-limit contract template,
- connector safety test suite,
- mock ERP / mock ticketing / mock document repository adapters.

## Still deferred

- real credential retrieval,
- live writes,
- production deployment,
- real ERP/email/ticket integrations.
