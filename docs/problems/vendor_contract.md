# Vendor & Contract Management

Problem
- Managing vendor performance, renewals, and SLAs across multiple clients is labor-intensive.

Agentic Solution (Pattern)
- Vendor & Contract Management Agent: monitor performance SLAs, renewal dates, automates renewal workflows and artifact linkage.
- Data & Integrations: procurement systems, contract repositories, client dashboards.
- HITL gating for renewals; dashboards for SLA health.

Success Metrics
- On-time renewal rate, SLA compliance, contract-cycle time reduction.

Risks and Mitigations
- Privacy/data sharing concerns; enforce data minimization and least-privilege access.

Example Prompts/Templates
- Renewal notification prompts, SLA monitoring prompts.
