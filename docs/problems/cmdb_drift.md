# CMDB Drift & Asset Lifecycle

Problem
- CMDB drift from dynamic environments; manual reconciliation expensive and error-prone.

Agentic Solution (Pattern)
- Asset Management & CMDB Agent: discovery, reconciliation, drift alerts, lifecycle automation (provision/decommission).
- Data & Integrations: asset discovery (network, cloud), CMDB, ticketing, change tools.
- HITL gating for critical lifecycle actions; metrics on drift rate and remediation time.

Success Metrics
- Drift rate, time-to-remediation, CMDB accuracy, reconciliation coverage.

Risks and Mitigations
- Incomplete discovery; mitigate with multi-source fusion and periodic reconciliation.

Example Prompts/Templates
- Drift detection prompts, reconciliation actions.
