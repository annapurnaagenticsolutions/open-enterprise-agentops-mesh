# Knowledge Gaps & Documentation

Problem
- Knowledge bases become stale; repetitive inquiries persist; new incidents fail to seed learnings.

Agentic Solution (Pattern)
- Knowledge & Documentation Agent: seed KB from incidents, changes, and runbooks; validate with usage metrics; automate publishing for high-priority articles.
- Data & Integrations: incident/change/runbooks, existing KB, collaboration tools.
- HITL gating for publish-worthy content; aging policies for updates.

Success Metrics
- KB accuracy, article adoption, time-to-publish for high-priority items.

Risks and Mitigations
- Propagation of incorrect guidance; require review for publishing; aging/remediation policies.

Example Prompts/Templates
- Article drafting prompts, publishing prompts, validation prompts.
