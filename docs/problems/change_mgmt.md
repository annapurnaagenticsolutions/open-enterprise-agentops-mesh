# Change & Release Management Bottlenecks

Problem
- RFC intake, risk assessment, CAB scheduling, and artifact tracking are manual and slow.

Agentic Solution (Pattern)
- Change & Release Governance Agent: RFC intake automation, risk scoring, CAB scheduling, artifact linkage, rollback hints.
- Data & Integrations: CI/CD metadata, CMDB, runbooks, artifacts.
- HITL gates for high-risk changes; rationale captured for auditability.

Success Metrics
- Time-to-RFC, CAB efficiency, change success rate, rollback occurrences.

Risks and Mitigations
- Inaccurate risk scoring; calibrate with domain experts; versioned adapters; automated tests.

Example Prompts/Templates
- RFC intake prompt, risk scoring rubric, CAB invitation prompts.
