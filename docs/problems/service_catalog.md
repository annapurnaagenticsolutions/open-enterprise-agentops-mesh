# Service Catalog & Offerings

Problem
- Service catalogs drift; clients require both standardization and customization to meet needs.

Agentic Solution (Pattern)
- Catalog & Offerings Agent: maintain living service catalog, flag gaps, propose new pre-packaged automation offerings aligned with client domains.
- Data & Integrations: CRM/PS, project management, tickets.
- HITL gating for new offerings; governance for standardization.

Success Metrics
- Catalog accuracy, offering adoption rate, time-to-market for new offerings.

Risks and Mitigations
- Balancing customization vs productization; define baseline templates and controlled customization.

Example Prompts/Templates
- New offering proposal prompts, catalog update prompts.
