# Product Thesis — Agentic Solutions Studio

The Agentic Solutions Studio is a portfolio-grade demonstration platform for Annapurna Agentic Solutions.

Its core claim is that useful agentic systems should be designed as governed workflows, not as generic chatbots. Each simulator demonstrates structured intake, scenario-specific output, human-review boundaries, safety checks, and a pilot path.

v0.5.1 improves the product thesis by separating the site into a brochure-style sequence:

1. Pitch
2. Simulators
3. Demo guide
4. Architecture
5. Roadmap

This makes the asset easier to present to clients, partners, or reviewers.
