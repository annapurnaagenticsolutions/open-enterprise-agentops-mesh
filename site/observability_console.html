why it matters

The trace ledger is not only technical logging. It is the evidence base for AgentOps governance.

  • Blocked actions reveal risky workflow design.
  • Policy decisions show recurring governance friction.
  • Provider usage supports cost and routing review.
  • Evidence IDs connect runtime behavior to approval artifacts.
  • Trace steps make agent execution explainable after the fact.

Next product direction
